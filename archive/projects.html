PROJECTS

As you might already know, I am very into the whole Maker Movement, and I love to diy things. I enjoy coding and software-related projects, game development, art, music, electronics, and building things. All of these were made at home or at my school FabLab. Some of my favorite makers/YouTubers are Evan and Katelyn, Mark Rober, Adam Savage, Bob from I Like to Make Stuff, and Simone Giertz. Check them out on YouTube, and check out my Instructables account, @ssmoogi, where I post detailed instructions on some of my favorite projects. Enjoy :)

T-SHIRT QUILT - JULY 2019

Over the Summer of 2019, I sewed together all of my mom's old shirts and my old shirts along with two old blankets to make something that I know I will keep for years to come. On there, you might see some shirts from my mom's work (Cisco)and my elementary school. This project is extremely simple, and through this is how I taught myself to use a sewing machine. WARCROSS JACKET - AUGUST 2019

I am so, so, SO in love with Marie Lu's books. They are all absolutely incredible! One of my number one favorites is Warcross. This jacket is painted with acrylic paint mixed with fabric medium. The back features part of a quote by the main character, Emika Chen, "Every locked door has a key, every problem has a solution." One sleeve shows the tatoo that Emika has in the novel, and the other arm states the author's name and the name of the company that created the game Warcross, Henka Games. This book is a definately recommended read, and this project was so much fun to make as well!
